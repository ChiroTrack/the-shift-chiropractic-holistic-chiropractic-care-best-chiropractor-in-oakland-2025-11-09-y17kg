Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how The Shift Chiropractic collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

The Shift Chiropractic ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and when using our services. This Privacy Policy explains our information practices, what types of information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect through our website, mobile applications, and when you visit our physical location or interact with our staff. By accessing or using The Shift Chiropractic's services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this document.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Health Information: Medical history, current symptoms, previous injuries, allergies, medications, and health conditions
  • Appointment Information: Appointment dates, times, preferences, and cancellation history
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method, and transaction history
  • Communications: Messages, feedback, and inquiries you send to us
  • Account Information: Username, password, and profile information if you create an account

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or use our services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and mobile device identifiers
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, and actions taken
  • Location Information: IP address, general geographic location, and GPS location (with permission)
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, including appointment scheduling platforms, payment processors, and insurance providers. We handle all third-party information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Service Delivery: Providing, maintaining, and improving our chiropractic services and customer experience
  • Appointment Management: Scheduling, confirming, and managing your appointments and treatment plans
  • Communication: Sending appointment reminders, treatment updates, and responding to your inquiries
  • Billing and Payment: Processing payments, sending invoices, and managing billing records
  • Health Care Operations: Maintaining medical records, coordinating care, and ensuring treatment continuity
  • Marketing and Promotions: Sending promotional materials, newsletters, and special offers (with your consent)
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with legal obligations, court orders, and regulatory requirements
  • Quality Improvement: Analyzing trends, gathering feedback, and improving our services
  • Fraud Prevention: Detecting, investigating, and preventing fraudulent activities and security breaches
  • Personalization: Customizing your experience and providing relevant content and recommendations

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on: (1) your consent, (2) performance of our contract with you, (3) our legitimate business interests, (4) compliance with legal obligations, and (5) protection of vital interests. For sensitive health information, we rely on your explicit consent and legal permissions under HIPAA and similar regulations.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our website. They are essential for website functionality and improving user experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality, security, and basic operations
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how you use our website to improve performance and user experience
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences, language settings, and customization choices
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity to deliver targeted advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness

Third-Party Tracking

We may allow third-party service providers, including analytics companies and advertising networks, to place cookies and tracking technologies on our website. These third parties collect information about your online activities to provide analytics, targeted advertising, and other services on our behalf.

Your Cookie Choices

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies, or be notified when a cookie is being set. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website. You can also opt out of targeted advertising through industry opt-out mechanisms.

Other Tracking Technologies

We may use web beacons, pixels, and similar technologies to track user behavior, measure campaign effectiveness, and personalize content. These technologies work in conjunction with cookies to provide a comprehensive understanding of user interactions with our services.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

When We Share Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Vendors who assist us with appointments, billing, marketing, and technical support
  • Insurance Companies: Your insurance provider for claims processing and coverage verification
  • Healthcare Providers: Other healthcare professionals involved in your care with your authorization
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement, courts, and government agencies as required by law
  • Business Partners: Partners for joint marketing initiatives and service offerings (with your consent)
  • Successors: In the event of merger, acquisition, or business transfer

Data Protection in Sharing

We require all third parties who receive your information to maintain appropriate security measures and use your information only for specified purposes. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Data Security

The Shift Chiropractic implements comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, and destruction. Our security practices include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure socket layer (SSL) technology for website communications
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy practices
  • Secure disposal of information when no longer needed

While we employ industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and notifying us of any unauthorized access.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Portability: Request a copy of your information in a portable format
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications and targeted advertising
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing of your information
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for information processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill legal obligations, and maintain business records. Retention periods vary depending on the type of information and the purposes for which we use it.

  • Medical Records: Retained for the duration of treatment and as required by law (typically 7 years)
  • Appointment Information: Retained for 3-5 years for business and legal purposes
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Website Analytics: Retained for up to 2 years for performance analysis
  • Marketing Information: Retained until you opt out or request deletion

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. You may request deletion of your information at any time, subject to legal and business requirements.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to us should contact us immediately. For children receiving treatment, we collect information from parents or legal guardians with appropriate consent and authority.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services that are not operated by The Shift Chiropractic. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services before providing your information.

We may also use third-party services for analytics, advertising, and other purposes. These third parties may collect information about your online activities across different websites and applications.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your home country. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may not have the same level of data protection.

We take appropriate safeguards to protect your information during international transfers, including using standard contractual clauses and implementing other legally recognized transfer mechanisms.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
