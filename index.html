About The Shift Chiropractic

Founded in 2014, The Shift Chiropractic emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to transform how Oakland residents experience healthcare through holistic, patient-centered chiropractic care. Our founder, Dr. Marcus Chen, built this practice on the belief that true wellness comes from addressing the root causes of pain and dysfunction, not just treating symptoms. Starting with a small clinic in downtown Oakland, we've grown into the region's most trusted chiropractic destination, serving thousands of patients and earning recognition as one of the Bay Area's leading wellness centers. Our growth is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence and our patients' trust in our methods.

At The Shift Chiropractic, our mission is to empower every patient to achieve optimal health through personalized, compassionate care. We believe in the body's innate ability to heal when properly aligned, and we're dedicated to removing the obstacles that prevent natural healing. Our core values—integrity, excellence, compassion, and innovation—guide every interaction and treatment decision. We're not just here to adjust spines; we're here to shift lives. By combining cutting-edge techniques with time-tested chiropractic principles, we create an environment where patients don't just recover from pain—they thrive and discover new levels of vitality they never thought possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our chiropractic services and treatment approach.

What conditions can chiropractic care treat?

Chiropractic care is effective for a wide range of conditions including back pain, neck pain, headaches, migraines, sciatica, whiplash injuries, sports injuries, carpal tunnel syndrome, and general wellness optimization. Our holistic approach addresses the underlying causes of pain and dysfunction, promoting natural healing and improved quality of life. Many patients also seek chiropractic care for preventative wellness and to maintain optimal spinal health.

Is chiropractic care safe? Are there any risks?

Yes, chiropractic care is very safe when performed by licensed, trained professionals like our team at The Shift. We conduct thorough assessments and use evidence-based techniques to minimize any risks. Serious complications are extremely rare. Most patients experience only minor, temporary side effects like mild soreness. We always discuss any potential risks during your consultation and customize your treatment plan to your specific health needs and conditions.

How many sessions will I need? How long does treatment typically take?

The number of sessions needed varies based on your specific condition, severity, and overall health. Some patients experience relief after just a few sessions, while others with chronic conditions may benefit from ongoing care. During your initial consultation, we'll assess your condition and provide a personalized treatment plan with realistic expectations. Many patients notice improvement within 2-4 weeks, though some conditions require longer-term management for optimal results.

Do you accept insurance? What are your payment options?

We work with most major insurance providers and can help you understand your coverage. We accept cash, credit cards, and electronic payments. We also offer flexible payment plans for patients without insurance or those with high deductibles. Our goal is to make quality chiropractic care accessible and affordable. Contact us directly to discuss your specific insurance and payment options.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive consultation where we'll discuss your health history, current symptoms, lifestyle, and wellness goals. We'll perform a thorough physical examination and may take X-rays if necessary. This allows us to accurately diagnose your condition and develop a personalized treatment plan. Our longer appointment times mean we take the time to really understand your needs. We'll explain everything in detail and answer all your questions before beginning any treatment.
